Designer

Designing is something I truly enjoy, and in a variety of formats. The art on this site, for example, is my own. I am also on multiple website design teams for different hackathons. But I also love to design things conceptually, be it for software or something else entirely.

Developer

I love being able to translate ideas into working software, websites, and other applications. Development excites me the most because I get to implement my ideas and designs into something useful. I have already had the privilege of helping people through medical software, and aim to continue to create useful products in a variety of fields.

Organizer

Getting involved in groups that share my passion is really important to me. I am an organizer for two different hackathons: BostonHacks, a hackathon run by BU students, and SheHacks Boston, the world's largest all-female and non-binary hackathon.
